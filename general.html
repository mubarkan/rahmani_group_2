ABOUT US

OUR STORY

2003 was the year when we established Rahmani Group. At that time we had a vision to change the way business is done in Afghanistan. Now, almost a decade later, we are on a path to pursue our dream revolutionizing the Corporate Industry in Afghanistan. Over the years, we have come a long way and have grown in capacity, performance, and image building.

Today, the country knows us as the first largest cement manufacturer and innovators of the industry. With growing competition and expectations of our stakeholders, we have not only taken unconventional approaches to our business operations, but are determined to promote economic growth and sustainable development of Afghanistan.

Regarding new business, we are focusing on the areas of industrial development. In a few years, we aim to produce cement and steel in the first place and then to the other areas of industrial revolution. Throughout all of our business, we are determined to enhance our sensitivity to market needs, in order to conduct flexible business activities with speed and agility while pursuing sustainable growth. As we consider our contributions to the sustainable development of society to be the basic Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR), our goal is to evolve business activities that are locally well established and that contribute to each area. At time of new global opportunities to contribute into this untapped but growing economy of Afghanistan.

We are stepping into consumer market by producing chicken for the bulk and household consumers. We are also in production planning phase of pharmaceutical and medicine for better and quality medicine production. We have largest supply chain network all over the country and linked offices in the neighbor countries with a complex and largest transportation network.

CEMENT MANUFACTURING

Rahmani Group was awarded a contract to set up a cement manufacturing facilities at Jabal-e-Saraj, Parwan. We were selected from a number of international bidders and curently we are in the final stage of contract negotiations. The contract is expected to be signed by March 2015. Javaid Jahoon Group and Oaseemi Group of Industries are our partners in this project. Oaseemi Group of Industries is an industry giant in corporate banking sector and fuel trading.

Numbers:

  • Project cost over $170 million
  • Group equity $60 million
  • Expected financing $110 million
  • Owned power generation 22 MW
  • Daily production 3,500 metric tons (360 days per year)

Partners:

  • Javaid Jahoon Group
  • Qaseemi Group of Industries

STEEL MANUFACTURING

Rahmani Group is working on setting up largest steel plant in Afghanistan, which will be located in Kabul. Feasability and paperwork are submited to Afghanistan Investment Support Agency (AISA), one of the principal licensing authorities in Afghanistan. The company incorporation is in progress. Also, we have submitted application for Power Connection in Breshna (National Power Generation & Distribution Company). Survey to be conducted on 10 February 2015.

Numbers:

  • Project cost over $15 million
  • Group equity $6 million
  • Expected financing $9 million
  • Owned power generation 22 MW
  • Daily production 200 metric tons

Rahmani Group is involved in construction projects as well. Currently a ten story commerical building is being raised in Dubai, UAE. Construction plans have been approved by the UAE government and the construction is scheduled to start in April of 2015.

POULTRY

Rahmani Group is investing in a set up of poultry production farm in Abu Dhabi. The farm is expected to output over 100 million eggs per year. The project requires $38 million in investments, all of which comes from Rahmani Group equity.
