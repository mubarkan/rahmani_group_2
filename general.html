CEMENT MANUFACTURING

Rahmani Group was awarded a contract for Cement Manufacturing at Jabal-e-Saraj, Parwan. We were selected from a number of international bidders, curently we are in the final stage of contract negotiations. The contract is expected to be signed by March 2015. The project costs $170 million, $60 million will be contributed by Rahmani Group, while othe $110 will be financed by external sources. the facility is expected to produce 3,500 metric tons of cement per day. Javaid Jahoon Group and Oaseemi Group of Industries are our partners in this project. Oaseemi Group of Industries is an industry giant in corporate banking sector and fuel trading.
